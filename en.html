The Infinite Wisdom That Shapes Everything
Truth
Has no color, no scent
Neither the echo of shout nor silence
Neither this nor that
Neither in the cup nor the wine

If you've reached this point,
Let me whisper in veils,
So none may hear the precious secret of the world:
What they said was but water’s shadow on stone

If you’ve stepped unknowing into the chaos of the winehouse,
Do not go further
Do not turn back
For both the seeker and the sought are lost.

Look at the sky— How solemn, how grand, how serene
Gazing upon the earth

So stay calm, Do not worry And do not ask of the world’s fate.
